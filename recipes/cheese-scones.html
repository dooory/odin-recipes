Cheese Scones

A quick and easy cheese scone recipe

These scones add an added savoury touch to your next morning tea.

12 servings with a prep and baking time of 20 minutes, quick and easy!

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups standard flour sifted
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • ¾ cup cheese grated
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp mustard powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 75g butter (or Olivani Spread)
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • extra milk for glazing
